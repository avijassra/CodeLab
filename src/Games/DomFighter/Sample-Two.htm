Bonner Scholars
The Bonner Scholars program is a scholarship and leadership development experience for students who demonstrate a sustained commitment to service and/or leadership in their schools and communities. To be eligible, a family must have an Expected Family Contribution (EFC) below $7,500 (approximately $60,000 annual family income). Selection is highly competitive.
